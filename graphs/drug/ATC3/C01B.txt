antiarrhythmics	are	drugs
drugs	used to treat	abnormal heart rhythms
antiarrhythmics	classified as	class I
class I	includes	sodium channel blockers
class I	used to	slow the conduction of electrical signals in the heart
antiarrhythmics	classified as	class III
class III	includes	potassium channel blockers
class III	used to	prolong the duration of the action potential in the heart
